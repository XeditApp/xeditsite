Xedit for macOS

Meet your next macOS IDE
for Cross Platform Development.
Free, open sourced and forever!

Xedit is a brand new IDE designed just for macOS.
It is tailored with Apple technologies and specific Apple APIs
to provide you with the best experience just like Xcode.
However, unlike Xcode, Xedit allows you to design your apps
for any platform other than Apple!
